Market Plan

Understanding our market, customers, and competition

Customer Segments

🏠 Individual Customers

Local community members seeking affordable, quality welding services. This group has been dissatisfied with poor-quality workmanship offered at high prices.

🏢 Commercial Customers

Transport companies, construction firms, and manufacturers requiring constant maintenance and fabrication of equipment.

🏫 Institutional Customers

Schools, colleges, hospitals, and government agencies requiring welding and fabrication services for infrastructure and equipment.

Competitive Advantage Strategies

  • Launch strong advertising campaigns targeting both individuals and institutions
  • Offer fair and competitive pricing compared to rivals
  • Provide credit facilities and discounts to attract loyal customers
  • Build a professional image with guaranteed quality services
  • Maintain personalized customer relationships by valuing feedback
  • Employ skilled and efficient staff to ensure high-quality workmanship

Pricing Strategy

Infinity Welding and Fabrication Ltd adopts a pricing approach that is fair to customers and profitable to the business.

  • Value-based pricing - reflecting durability and craftsmanship
  • Tiered pricing - for small, medium, and large projects
  • Loyalty discounts - for repeat customers
  • Referral rewards - customers who refer new clients receive service discounts
  • Flexible payment options - for institutions and bulk clients
