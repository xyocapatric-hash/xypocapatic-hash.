About Us

Learn more about Infinity Welding and Fabrication Ltd

Business Description

Infinity Welding and Fabrication Ltd is a startup enterprise to be established in Thika, strategically positioned along the busy Thika-Nairobi Highway. The company specializes in delivering high-quality welding and fabrication services, offering durable metal structures and customized solutions to meet the needs of both individuals and businesses.

Why Choose Us?

  • Strategic Location: 500m from Thika Town Centre along Thika-Nairobi Highway
  • Expert Team: Skilled and qualified welders with professional training
  • Quality Guarantee: High standards of quality, strength, and durability
  • One-Stop Solution: Fabrication services plus supply of essential metal products
  • Security: Police post within 100m of the premises

Form of Ownership

The business operates as a sole proprietorship, wholly owned by Mr. Patrick Nzioka, who assumes full responsibility for management and overall operations.

Justification of Opportunity

  • Strong customer demand in Thika and surrounding areas
  • Opportunity to enhance specialized skills
  • Relatively low competition in the region
  • Job stability and security
  • Customers currently travel 45km to Nairobi for quality metal works

Business Goals

  • Deliver high-quality welding services
  • Extend services to communities
  • Achieve sustainable profitability
  • Generate local employment
  • Contribute to regional development

Industry

Operating within the manufacturing and fabrication sector. The company is committed to adopting modern techniques to stay current with evolving industry practices.
